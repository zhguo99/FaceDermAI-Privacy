FaceDermAI Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2023

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how FaceDermAI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our mobile application ("FaceDermAI").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

Information We Collect

Personal Data: FaceDermAI processes facial images that you provide to the application for analysis. These images are:

  • Processed securely and are not permanently stored unless specifically saved by you
  • Not shared with third parties except as described in this policy
  • Used solely for the purpose of providing facial and skin analysis

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to help improve our application, including:

  • App performance metrics
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Error reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To perform facial and skin analysis as requested by you
  • To improve and optimize our application
  • To detect and address technical issues

Data Transmission and Third-Party Services

When using cloud AI features, your facial images are securely transmitted to Google's Gemini AI service for processing. All data is encrypted during transmission.

We use Google's Gemini AI in accordance with their terms of service and privacy policy.

Data Storage

Analysis results are stored locally on your device. Images are not permanently stored by our application unless specifically saved by you.

API keys that you provide are stored securely in the iOS Keychain.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as:

  • The right to access information we have about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to our processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
